    Welcome to Feedback Foward!

    What is Feedback Foward?

    Feedback Foward is a Website where both highly-skilled and entry level individuals can learn from one another.

    -Our website allows users to post project they are working on, or need help with.

    -You can post about a given topic for a focus area of your choice (Game Design, Coding, Music Production, Web Development... etc.), and get help from individuals who may have a greater insight on a given issue, project, or task.

    -Likewise, you can also help individuals who may need help and feedback on a given issue, project, or task.

    -You can also build your profile, to showcase your various accomplishments, skills, expertise, and details about yourself!

    -Feedback Foward aims to provide users with the ability to learn new skills, and areas of interest, whilst avoiding the banter that comes along with traditional forum websites.


    Note: The Feedback Foward Website & Web Servers are currently still under development, until further notice. -T.K.
